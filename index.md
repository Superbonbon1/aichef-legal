## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you sign in to AI-Chef (via Google Sign-In or email), we collect:
- Email address
- Display name (if provided)
- Profile photo URL (if using Google Sign-In)

This information is used solely for authentication and is stored securely by Firebase Authentication.

### 2.2 Recipe Data
All recipes you create, save, import, or generate are stored **locally on your device** using SQLite. This data includes:
- Recipe titles, ingredients, and instructions
- Tags and categories you assign
- Favorite recipes you mark
- Custom notes you add

**Important:** Your recipe data is stored only on your device and is not uploaded to our servers or any cloud service.

### 2.3 Camera and Photo Library Access
AI-Chef requests access to your device's camera and photo library to allow you to:
- Take photos of dishes to associate with recipes
- Select images from your photo library to add to recipes

We do **not** upload, store, or process your photos on any external server. Photos you add to recipes remain stored locally on your device.

### 2.4 AI-Generated Content
When you use AI features (generate, refine, adapt, or scale recipes), we send the following to our AI processing service:
- Your recipe prompt or request
- Selected preferences (dietary restrictions, servings, persona)
- The recipe content being modified (for refine/adapt/scale operations)

We do **not** send any personally identifiable information (PII) to AI services. The AI service processes your request and returns the generated content, which is then stored locally on your devic[...]  

### 2.5 Analytics and Diagnostics
We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the App, including:
- App version and device type
- Feature usage patterns (which screens are visited, which actions are taken)
- Error logs and crash reports
- Network latency measurements

This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users. We do not track your location.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the AI-Chef service
- Authenticate your identity when you sign in
- Process AI recipe generation requests
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Develop new features based on usage patterns

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
Your recipe data, favorites, tags, and photos are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data remains on your device and is not synced to any cloud service.

### 4.2 Authentication
Your account credentials are managed securely by Firebase Authentication. We do not have access to your password.

### 4.3 Data Transmission
All communication between the App and our servers uses HTTPS encryption. AI processing requests are transmitted securely and contain no personally identifiable information.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information only in the following circumstances:
- **Service Providers:** We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and cloud functions. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Deletion
- All your recipe data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.
- You can sign out of your account at any time in the app settings.
- You can request deletion of your Firebase account by contacting us.

### 6.2 Camera and Photo Permissions
You can revoke camera and photo library permissions at any time through your device's settings. The app will continue to work, but you won't be able to add photos to recipes.

### 6.3 Analytics Opt-Out
AI-Chef respects your device's system-level analytics settings. If you disable analytics sharing in your device settings, we will honor that preference.
